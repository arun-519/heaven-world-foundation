Our Profile

Hindusthan College of Arts & Science (HICAS) was started by Hindusthan Educational & Charitable Trust in 1998 with the primary objective of providing quality education for all students irrespective of caste and creed. Embraced by serene nature, world-class infrastructure, and an excellent learning environment, HICAS stands as an outstanding institution known for academic excellence with the following Vision and Mission.

Vision

To provide world-class education to students to face global challenges and to inculcate the latest trends in technological advancement.

To cater to the needs of environmental and ethical values in the minds of students to become good citizens and entrepreneurs.

Mission

The mission of the college is to pursue a philosophy of perpetual acquisition of knowledge.

The important policy is to provide value-based education and to bring out the hidden potential in students, equipping them to approach life with optimism.
