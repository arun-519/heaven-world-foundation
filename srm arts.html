SRM Institutions

SRM Group of Educational Institutions for Higher Learning functions under the aegis of the 4-decade old “Valliammai Society”. The Society was founded for promoting the cause of Quality Education, by Dr T. R. Paarivendhar, an Academician and Educationist. The SRM Group runs a network of more than 27 Institutions and is one of the largest educational groups in the country.

SRM Group

The branches of study include Engineering and Technology, Management, Medical/ Dental/ Life and Bio-Sciences/ Para Medical and Allied Medical Sciences; Hospitality and Arts & Science. Contributions made by The SRM Group has been consistent over the years through 'Educational Institutions' and 'Health Care Services', are well recognized throughout the country.

Who are we

SRM Trichy Arts and Science College, affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli was started in the academic year 2018-2019. The college is in continuous pursuit of educational excellence by providing a holistic learning environment for students with state of the art infrastructure & facilities.
